Every Cup Starts With A Coconut

From the milk in our filter coffee to the cream in our gelato — Thenga Cafe turns Kerala's most generous fruit into something worth slowing down for.

View Menu Our Story

Grown Along Our Backwaters, Poured Into Your Cup

Every coconut we use is sourced from smallholder farms along Kerala's backwaters. We press our own coconut milk daily, toast our own coconut flakes, and let the fruit — not syrups or shortcuts — carry the flavour in everything we make.
