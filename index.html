Our story

Built on the Coast, Poured in the Cup

Thenga means coconut in Malayalam — and it's the thread running through everything we make. We grew up watching coconuts get grated for chutney, shaved for sweets, and pressed for milk. That tradition lives here, one cup and one plate at a time.

Every batch of milk, every scoop of gelato, and every plate on our menu begins with coconut sourced from farms along Kerala's backwaters.
